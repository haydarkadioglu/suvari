# Rules of Engagement
- Only test targets you own or have explicit permission to test
- Do not use destructive payloads (DROP TABLE, rm -rf, etc.)
- Rate-limit requests to avoid DoS
- Stop testing if target shows signs of instability
- Report all findings regardless of severity

# Scope
- Focus on: Injection, XSS, SSRF, Broken Authentication, Broken Authorization
- Secondary: Information Disclosure, Security Misconfiguration
- Out of scope: Social engineering, physical attacks, client-side attacks requiring user interaction
